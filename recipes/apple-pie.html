Apple Pie

Description

Flakey, buttery crust with juicy sweet apple filling!

Ingredients

Dough

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Egg

Filling

  • Apples
  • Lemon Juice
  • Sugar
  • Unsalted Butter
  • Ground Cinnamon
  • Ground Nutmeg
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the dough.
  2. Form the dough into a disk and refridgerate for 1 hour.
  3. Make the filling
  4. Cook the filling on a large skillet
  5. Separate and cool the apples from the liquid and toss with spices. Reduce the liquid and add it to the apples. Set asside to cool.
  6. Cut dough in half and roll to line a pie pan.
  7. Add filling to pie.
  8. Cover the pie with the rest of the dough and seal the edges.
  9. Refridgerate for 15 minutes.
  10. Bake until golden brown, usually about 50 minutes 375 degrees F.
  11. Let cool before enjoying!
