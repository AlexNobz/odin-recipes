Lasagna

Description

Spicy pork lasagna with ricotta and parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • Spicy Italian Pork
  • Chopped Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Chopped Garlic
  • Dried Basil
  • Noodles
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat in a skillet until browned.
  2. Add the chopped onion to the skillet and cook until translucent.
  3. Add tomato sauce, dried basil, and chopped garlic to the skillet and cook until bubbling.
  4. Cook the noodles in salted water until just under done.
  5. Mix the ricotta and parmesan cheese togther with the eggs.
  6. Assemble the lasagna in layers of sauce, noodles, and cheese in a dish.
  7. Cover with foil and bake at 375 for 50 minutes, removing the foil for the last 10 minutes of baking.
